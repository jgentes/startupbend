I Left Intel in the South Sound several years ago. We now call Bend home. Now, I have a startup that is fast becoming very fun!

We haven’t completely bailed on Seattle. We still have a townhouse in Magnolia we use a few months a year (definitely July and August most years!) and rent out as a short-term furnished rental the remainder of the time. I still do all of our favorite activities with our Seattle friends like climbing in the Cascades, rock climbing at Leavenworth, trail running at Tiger and across the street at Discovery Park, and the I-90 hikes. And there’s all the great ethnic food that you just can’t get in Bend.

We love calling Bend home, but with some good extended trips to the Emerald City it is, perhaps, like “eating my Paseo’s pork sandwich and having it too.”
