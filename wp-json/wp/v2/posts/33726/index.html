There’s no doubt that Bend Research has played an integral part in the formation of Bend’s burgeoning bioscience and entrepreneurial community. You can hear more about the company’s history at a unique panel discussion hosted by BendBio<\/a> Wednesday, April 13.<\/p>\n

Drew Bledsoe, former NFL player, current Bendite and owner of Bledsoe Capital Group and Doubleback Winery, will talk with Rod Ray, former CEO of Bend Research and now a member of the company’s scientific and business advisory board. The discussion will focus on Central Oregon’s entrepreneurial spirit and Bend Research’s contributions to Bend.<\/p>\n

Ray began at Bend Research in 1983 and held various positions with the company, which was acquired by New Jersey-based Capsugel in 2013<\/a>. Bledsoe is no stranger to entrepreneurship himself, launching his Walla Walla-based winery and serving on the board of Bend-based startup whurk<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Dr. Knute Buehler, local surgeon, business owner and state representative, will kick the evening off with a presentation, \u201cWhy Bend is a Model City for Entrepreneurial Activity in Oregon.\u201d They’ll be networking with other bioscience and entrepreneurial types, appetizers and a no host bar.<\/p>\n

The details:<\/p>\n

BendBio Tech Forum
\nApril 13, 5-7:30pm, Wille Hall (COCC Campus)
