Does your child want to learn to code? Do you want to spark an interest in your kids in computer science? Well, you’re in luck. Bend-based Manzama is introducing its first “Cool Kids Code” course, a FREE six-week class for kids ages 8 to 12.<\/p>\n

Enrollment\u00a0began today<\/a>. Classes start Aug. 14 and run until October.<\/p>\n

The course is a homegrown attempt to address the general lack of computer science education available to kids. According to this sad pie chart, just a sliver of kids graduate college with a computer science degree, despite the\u00a0plethora of jobs in the field. (Side note: Try to find a software company here or elsewhere in Oregon that isn’t looking for good developers.)<\/p>\n
