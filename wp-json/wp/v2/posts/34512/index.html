The Cascade Angels team is growing. The Bend-based angel fund announced today that startup veteran Robert Pease will be the fund’s community advisor as well as a member of the investment committee.<\/p>\n

\n

From the news release:<\/p>\n

Pease currently leads the Pipeline Performance group at Seattle-based B2B sales and marketing consulting firm Heinz Marketing<\/a>, helping companies improve execution and accelerate revenue.\u00a0He was previously CEO of LoopFuse, a marketing automation software company acquired by Salesfusion, Vice President of Marketing at Gist, a social CRM software company acquired by BlackBerry, and has held a variety of senior marketing and product roles in four other venture-backed technology startups.<\/p>\n

Pease began his career as a management consultant with Accenture\u2019s Global Communications and Technology practice based in Washington, DC. He holds a BBA in Finance from the University of Georgia and an MBA from the University of Tennessee.<\/p><\/blockquote>\n
