Stridebox<\/a><\/strong>, a subscription box service for runners, has hired five employees in the past six months. Onboard Dynamics<\/strong> <\/a>was psyched to hire its first full-time mechanical engineer in April. The Handsel<\/a><\/strong>, an app that makes it easier to buy, sell and swap on Facebook, will be presenting at next week’s Pub Talk. You can still buy tickets<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Next Odysys<\/a><\/strong>, a Bend-based company that provides reservation and marketing software for boutique hotels,\u00a0continues to hire<\/a>. The company is looking for an inside sales executive<\/a> as well as three developers.<\/p>\n

Also\u00a0Agency Revolution<\/a><\/strong>, which provides marketing automation software for insurance professionals, hired the delightful Katie Smith as its Director of Marketing. Smith comes to Agency with 12 years of marketing experience including as an independent consultant, CRM analyst for Mt. Bachelor and director of grassroots marketing and sales with Ragnar Relays.<\/p>\n

And Kidrunner<\/strong><\/a>, which makes an innovative running stroller that you pull instead of push, is hosting a demo party and taking pre-orders tonight (Wednesday, May 20th) at Footzone.
