If you haven’t heard, Central Oregon is the hot spot for unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV). Collectively, we want to bring a large portion of this massive industry to our state in the next 20 years. Leading that effort are the folks from SOAR Oregon and the Cascade Chapter of AUVSI. Their upcoming conference on Oct 1 and 2 will discuss the future of this technology under the event banner of “Working Together in the National Airspace System.”<\/p>\n

The event will mark the first conference at a UAS Test Range in the Pacific Northwest and include prominent industry speakers, flight demonstrations, technical sessions, a job fair, and business networking. For the full flyer, see below, or to register online,\u00a0click here.<\/a><\/p>\n
