The Bend Venture Conference and the Bend Outdoor Worx conferences are coming up — and the news about the startups participating in these pitch contests has been coming fast and furious too.<\/p>\n

So first up, the BOW folks announced the companies that will be competing in BOW Breakout on Oct. 17th. The six finalists include a nice mix of startups at different stages and businesses from Bend and beyond. The companies are vying for\u00a0$15k in cash grants and Business Oregon Financing Award up to $100,000 for Oregon companies, according to the BOW news release.<\/p>\n

For the BreakOut Award, early stage category, the finalists are:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Mystic Devices<\/a>\u00a0<\/strong>(Eagle, CO), Trail adaptive bike lighting system.<\/li>\n
  • Sew Go Outdoors<\/a>\u00a0<\/strong>(Bend, OR) Lightweight adventure gaitors.<\/li>\n
  • Lacuda\u00a0<\/strong>(Bend, OR),\u00a0new resistance training swimsuits for competitive female swimmers and triathletes.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    For the Ready-To-Roll Award, growth stage category, the finalists are:<\/p>\n

      \n
    • Heather’s Choice <\/a><\/strong>(Anchorage, AK) Light-weight backpacking food.<\/li>\n
    • Ombraz Sunglasses\u00a0<\/strong>(Seattle, WA)\u00a0<\/strong>A revolutionary pair of sunglasses with a built-in adjustable cord that replaces the temple arms that hold them comfortably and securely on your face.<\/li>\n
    • Bluey Merino<\/a>\u00a0<\/strong>\u00a0(NSW, Aus) Active and outdoor merino wool clothing brand. Farm-to-consumer direct model.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      Tickets for BOW Breakout are available in advance at\u00a0TowerTheatre.org<\/a>\u00a0and at the door.<\/p>\n

      • Ella & Oak<\/a>\u00a0(Bend, Ore.) \u2013 An online bridal marketplace for plus-size brides bringing the \u201ctry and buy\u201d experience home to consumers before making the big purchase.<\/li>\n
