The competition is heating up around these parts as startups vie for the chance to present at the Bend Venture Conference next month. Economic Development for Central Oregon has selected the 10 concept stage companies that will compete Sept. 25th at the EDCO PubTalk<\/a>.<\/p>\n

There the esteemed audience members (and general yahoos like me) will vote for the five companies that get to proceed to BVC<\/a>, where they get the chance to compete for\u00a0$10,000 prize provided by BendBroadband.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe 10 concept stage contenders are a diverse set, representing a variety of industries including outdoor recreation, bioscience, consumer products, high-tech, and agriculture,\u201d said Nate LiaBraaten, BVC concept stage coordinator. \u201cIt will be important for the voting audience, in their evaluation, to really consider which five ideas can successfully launch and scale into a profitable company.\u201d<\/p>\n

Here are the contenders, in alphabetical order:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Beliggle <\/strong>is a\u00a0service exchange mobile app, an idea that came out this year\u2019s OSU-Cascades entrepreneurship class.<\/li>\n
  • Bird Dog Bioventures<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0is a veterinary\/human biology start-up, researching and licensing underserved areas of animal health.<\/li>\n
  • Cairn<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0is monthly subscription box for outdoor enthusiasts. The company introduces its subscribers to new brands and products while also collecting market data for the companies they work with.<\/li>\n
  • Free Range Equipment<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0manufactures light, durable, simple, functional backpacks for the performance focused athlete.<\/li>\n
  • FriendlySniff.com<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0is an online tool that organizes and simplifies the pet owner\u2019s life, from adoptions, training, housing, shopping, and other local services.<\/li>\n
  • KidRunner<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0is reinventing running and exploring with kids hands and arms free. The company has created high performance kid carrier that runners\/walkers\/hikers can pull hands-free, instead of push.<\/li>\n
  • Odysys, LLC<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0provides a complete digital marketing platform for hotels. The software aims to simplify direct hotel bookings by reducing guest acquisition costs, increasing revenue, and encouraging repeat guests with one simple platform.<\/li>\n
  • Party Gorilla, Inc.<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0is an app that provides users with information about their favorite bars and clubs. The app helps users know what’s happening, when and where and gives bars the tools to help get their customers get there.<\/li>\n
  • Ubimodo, LLC<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0is creating an app that helps keeps people safe by notifying the authorities when things go wrong, such as the user not returning at a designated time from an outdoor adventure. The system initiates a series of escalating responses up to law enforcement\/search and rescue involvement.<\/li>\n
  • Volcano Veggies LLC<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0grows organic vegetables and fish indoors with proprietary biotechnology solutions, providing cold-weather communities with fresh, nutritious local food year-round.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
