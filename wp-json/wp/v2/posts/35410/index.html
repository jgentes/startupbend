TechfestNW is fast approaching and Bend startups are ready to make a strong showing. The conference, which runs April 5th and 6th in Portland, includes a pitch competition aptly named PitchfestNW<\/a>. More than 80 founders will pitch their startups, including at least six from Bend. Who are they? Glad you asked. TechfestNW<\/a> attendees and the esteemed judging panel will get to hear from:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Locals Compass<\/a>, a marketplace that connects travelers with locals for personalized itineraries.<\/li>\n
  • Kidcareshare<\/a>, an app lets you instantly schedule and trade childcare with friends and family.<\/li>\n
  • BladeRunner Energy<\/a>,\u00a0a micro-hydro technology based on biomimicry that harnesses renewable energy from the natural flow of water.<\/li>\n
  • Olitis<\/a>, the creator of iiShakers, which are salt and pepper shakers with an integrated, smart lighting technology.<\/li>\n
  • Life Exec<\/a>, a life management resource dedicated to ensuring communication between you and your loved ones in any situation.<\/li>\n
  • Investipro<\/a>, a cloud-based platform that guides HR professionals through workplace investigations.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    These Bend-based founders will pitch their startup on Thursday. The judges, which include representatives from many NW venture and angel funds, will then select 5 startups to present to the entire conference on Friday. The winner will receive a package of prizes that has yet to be announced, exposure to investors and general bragging rights for life.<\/p>\n

    PitchFestNW is just one part of TechFestNW. The two-day conference also includes speakers, workshops and many exhibitors. If that sounds like your jam, then get your tickets<\/a> and head over the mountain.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
