Bend-based InvestiPro <\/a>announced today that it’s closed a seed round of funding from Cascade Angels. Founded by Dana Barbato, the startup provides a cloud-based platform that automates workplace investigations.<\/p>\n

Dana has deep experience in human resources, and the software is “designed to simplify and automate workplace investigation from start to finish, it keeps the process timely, consistent, and above all compliant,” according to the news release<\/a>.<\/p>\n

\n

\u201cAsk any HR professional, and they\u2019ll say they dread the term workplace investigation,\u201d Dana says. \u201cInvestiPro transforms those investigations into positive experiences.\u201d<\/p>\n<\/blockquote>\n

The company launched last year, and now counts clients from small businesses to Fortune 5000 companies. InvestiPro didn’t say how much Cascade Angels invested. It’s the Bend-based angel fund’s 16th investment overall, and third investment in 2017.<\/p>\n
