Summer school is officially in session for the inaugural BendPoly class.<\/p>\n

Sixteen students, a mix of undergrads and graduate students from University of Oregon, OSU-Cascades, George Fox, Hawaii Pacific and Cornell, started a six-week deep dive into digital marketing today right here in the high desert.<\/p>\n

The idea is give them some practical skill<\/a>s that will complement their college education — and help them find jobs once they graduate. BendPoly<\/a> was founded by Bruce Cleveland, a Bay Area tech veteran and venture capitalist who now calls Bend home. Cari Baldwin, CEO of Bend-based Bluebird Strategies, serves as the school’s chief instruction officer.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

In addition to Baldwin, this summer’s students will benefit from a roster of experienced guest instructors from companies including Google, Zendesk, Content4Demand, Obility, and Host Analytics. Today’s keynote speakers were Cleveland and Jon Miller, co-founder of Marketo and current CEO of Engagio.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The students will spend their summer both learning in the classroom at the 1001 Tech Center (which also houses BendTECH’s co-working space) and applying their new found digital marketing skills to projects for local companies.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
