There’s a new event happening this summer and it’s one you don’t want to miss! Discover what makes Central Oregon so innovative at the first\u00a0Innovation Day on July 18th at OSU-Cascades.<\/p>\n

The event is coordinated by Opportunity Knocks and supported by a host of organizations including TAO, OSU-Cascades, The Bend Chamber and BendTECH. Here’s more from the organizers:<\/p>\n

Innovation Day 2017 is a first-time Bend event that, similar to TEDxBend, PPP, and the Bend Venture Conference, will become a permanent fixture in Bend\u2019s collaborative, people-driven culture.<\/p>\n

The event will feature a speed-dating format allowing you to meet, hear and chat with our region\u2019s leading innovators. Table discussion facilitators will include Scott Allan from HydroFlask talking with you about his company\u2019s unusual culture, Shannon Keith sharing Sudara\u2019s social impact concept, Jim Schell and Roger Lee discussing the role of collaboration in Central Oregon\u2019s entrepreneurial ecosystem, and Preston Callicott and Kelly Sparks will discuss the new Innovation District. And there’s so much more!<\/p>\n

Learn, contribute and be inspired! Space is limited so register today<\/a> to reserve your spot!<\/p><\/blockquote>\n
