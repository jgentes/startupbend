F<\/strong>BendTECH Coworking presents the third annual Startup Resource Fair, scheduled for 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. on Thursday, June 6, 2019 at the 1001 Tech Center. This informative and fun event puts all the resources you need to start or grow a business in one location. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

The fair features 30 exhibitors from around Oregon\nincluding: <\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Angel Funds, Seed Funds, and Banks<\/li>
  • Business\/Founder Mentor Groups, Accelerators and\nBootcamps<\/li>
  • Small Business Assistance Programs<\/li>
  • Core Professional Services <\/li>
  • Educational Organizations and Resources <\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    The goal is to give entrepreneurs an easy way to learn about all the available resources in our community and make it easy to connect with them. The fair is for all industries\u2014there are resources for tech, outdoor, food and health companies and more. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Each exhibitor will have a table, and attendees can learn more about the organization, meet key people and ask questions. People can also enjoy a free beer, courtesy of Deschutes Brewery, hang out on the patio and socialize with Bend\u2019s startup community.

    Admission is free. Attendees who register will receive a ticket for a free drink. Please get more information and register online at bendtech.com\/startupresourcefair<\/a>. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    The BendTECH Startup Resource Fair is sponsored by St.\nCharles Health System, First Republic Bank, Business Oregon and the 1001 Tech\nCenter. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
