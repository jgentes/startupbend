If you have the chance to attend last year’s Bend Venture Conference, then you probably saw the wonder that was LuDela candles<\/a> — smart, real-flame candles that you can turn on with a remote (or with your Alexa). Well the company also caught the attention of Techstars Boulder, and will be joining the accelerator’s 13th class in Colorado.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

LuDela is part of the 10-company cohort<\/a>, which includes six startups from Colorado. Founder Jamie Bianchini is headed to Boulder for the three-month program. His company is currently housed at BendTECH, and Jamie plans to return Central Oregon after Techstars wraps up.<\/p>\n

Jamie took second place at unConference and was a BVC early stage finalist. He started his company after experiencing a candle fire while on a bike trip<\/a>\u00a0in western Africa. He wanted to develop a safer candle that still offered that real-life experience.<\/p>\n

Congratulations to Jamie and the LuDela team.<\/p>\n
