By Sean Leslie
\nFounder, Cascade Cadence\u00a0<\/a><\/p>\n

The annual Bend Outdoor Worx (BOW) Breakout<\/a> event started with free beer and ended with a live punk band rattling the foundation of downtown\u2019s Tower Theatre. Between the beer and the music, BOW awarded more than $15,000 to young outdoor product companies, helping them to continue their growth while at the same time giving them an opportunity to pitch their businesses to an audience of hundreds, many of whom were of potential investors.<\/p>\n

“Tonight, the road to success runs right through Bend, OR,\u201d cheered emcee Gary Bracelin to an energized crowd as he kicked off the event, 10 Barrel beer in hand.<\/p>\n
