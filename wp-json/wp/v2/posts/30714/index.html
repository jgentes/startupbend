OEN held it’s second annual summit\u00a0<\/a>Friday at the Kennedy School in Portland. Emcee Terry “Starbucker” St. Marie offered 10 leadership lessons<\/a> relayed by some of the state’s most successful entrepreneurs.<\/p>\n

\n

There was a diverse group of businesses represented, including restaurants, social media platforms,non-profits, clothing, bio-med, farming, software, and bicycles.<\/p>\n

While there was a wide diversity in business type, there was a real commonality in the lessons these entrepreneurs shared \u00a0– the \u201chard knocks\u201d leadership lessons.<\/p>\n

These were realizations that came directly from their experiences \u00a0– revelations that pushed them past being just a \u201cboss\u201d, but into the higher strata of more human leader.<\/p>\n<\/blockquote>\n
