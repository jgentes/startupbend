Have you ever been lost or stuck somewhere and had that terrible realization that no one knows where you are or when you\u2019re supposed to be home? The founders of Bend-based startup Ubimodo are creating a software app to prevent just that.<\/p>\n

Ubimodo is Latin for where — now, and the company tagline is “Panic is Not a Strategy.” (Note to self: Rethink all strategies). BendTECH sat down with co-founder Scott Warner as part of our #50startups project<\/a> to learn more about Ubimodo, how it helps find lost people and why working on the Terminator movies was harder than founding a startup.<\/p>\n
