Cairn, the Bend-based subscription box company for outdoor lovers, is nearing its first birthday — and it’s full-speed ahead. In the past few months the startup has hired brand expert Bill Inman, previously with ExOfficio, Merrell and Nike, and set up new nonprofit partnerships. Along the way, the company has also garnered a few thousand box subscribers.<\/p>\n

Thanks to the latter, the operation has officially outgrown CEO and founder Rob Little’s garage.<\/p>\n
