Hang onto your hats. Because\u00a0Economic Development for Central Oregon<\/a> (EDCO), host of the Bend Venture Conference<\/a> (BVC), announced today the five growth stage companies that will pitch at the conference later this month. The startups are vying for $500,000 in investments and cash prizes.<\/p>\n

The BVC investors selected the five finalists from a pool of more than 60. They are:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • HoneyComb<\/a>: Transforming agriculture with on-demand data services and drone technology.<\/li>\n
  • NemaMetrix<\/a>: Offering an early stage drug discovery platform that reduces costs 100x by increasing the success of identifying drug candidates early.<\/li>\n
  • Odysys<\/a>: Helping hotels drive more direct bookings at a lower cost by allowing hoteliers to connect, manage and track digital marketing efforts with one simple platform.<\/li>\n
  • Perfect Company<\/a>: Revolutionizing cooking with a\u00a0connected scale and interactive recipe solution.<\/li>\n
  • Scratch-it<\/a>: Providing\u00a0a Software as a Service (SaaS) marketing solution that manages a new type of content called “reveal,” which consistently improves engagement by 10x.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    \u201cThis group of finalists represents a breadth of industries, ranging from software to the Internet of Things to drones,\u201d said Brian Vierra, Venture Catalyst for EDCO. \u201cI\u2019m really impressed with how far some of these companies have come in a very short period of time.\u201d<\/p>\n

    Applicants for the conference came from up and down the West Coast and beyond including Seattle, Portland and Bend. Fun fact: You might recognize Odysys as one of our #50startups #inbend<\/a>. The BVC takes place on Oct. 15 and Oct. 16. Tickets are still available<\/a>.<\/p>\n
