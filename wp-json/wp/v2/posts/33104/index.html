Do you want the chance to win money for exercising, eating healthy or even sleeping? KrowdFit, a Bend-based fitness tech startup, has created a wellness reward program that offers cash prizes — with winners drawn at random — to participants.<\/p>\n

The company recently announced that it had partnered with Oregon Metro<\/a> to offer its corporate wellness program to government personnel. Beginning this month, Metro’s 1,600-plus employees\u00a0will be eligible for KrowdFit’s weekly, monthly and quarterly cash rewards of up to $5,000.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

However, CEO Jim Miller<\/a> says the fun — and prizes — aren’t reserved for corporate employees. Individuals can participate too. The startup’s release of its iOS<\/a> and Droid apps<\/a> earlier this month make it that much easier.<\/p>\n
