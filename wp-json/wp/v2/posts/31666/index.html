Molly Scofield made a last minute decision to enter the pitch contest at the unConference last month and handily won the wildcard spot at the Bend Venture Conference. Her polished pitch for The Handsel, a mobile app that aims to improve how people buy, sell and swap used goods online, didn\u2019t win the $10,000 concept prize, but it did garner some significant attention from the audience and investors alike.<\/p>\n

Scofield, a senior vice president at Global Strategies International, co-founded the company with Dave Mullan, a former Microsoft employee and Seattle startup veteran, nearly two years ago. BendTECH caught up with Scofield this week to learn more about Scofield\u2019s vision for the company and what spurred her impromptu unConference presentation.<\/p>\n
