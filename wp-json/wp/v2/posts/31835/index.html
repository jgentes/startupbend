Julia Junkin found herself at a juncture a few years ago. A licensed artist, she\u2019d created designs for major retail brands via her ceramic company with product being sold throughout the country. But she wanted to change course and do something new.<\/p>\n

The result: JUJU, an app Junkin created that allows individuals to essentially remix existing art from artists to create their own artistic expressions sent through postcards, text messages, e-cards and more. BendTECH recently sat down with Junkin to learn more about JUJU, its pending SXSW debut and the opportunity the app presents brands for customer engagement.<\/p>\n
