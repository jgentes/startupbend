You know when you want to tell someone two exciting things at once and it’s hard to decide which one should go first? Welcome to this post. First off, Lora DiCarlo, the sextech startup based right here in Bend, has secured $2 million in seed funding. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

The money comes from both new and existing investors and makes use of federal Opportunity Zones (hang with me while we talk about taxes for a minute, because then we’ll talk about ROBOTIC SEX TOYS). <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Opportunity Zones are rural or economically distressed areas that the IRS believes could benefit from some long-term investment. Both of Lora DiCarlo’s locations in Corvallis and Bend are designated as Opportunity Zones, which means that the company’s investors receive additional tax benefits under the federal program.

From the Lora DiCarlo news release: <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u201cI invested because Lora DiCarlo is positioned at the intersection of sexual health research and cutting-edge robotic technology creating new products that help everyone have more satisfying sexual interactions,\u201d says new Lora DiCarlo investor Richard Kado, President of Kado Family Inc.  \u201cAdditionally, Lora DiCarlo\u2019s commitment to low-income and underserved communities demonstrates an exceptional level of corporate social responsibility while also providing meaningful tax advantages for investors.\u201d<\/p><\/blockquote>\n\n\n\n

The company chose to locate in Opportunity Zones to spur economic development and job creation in these historically distressed communities. Lora DiCarlo has created 14 new full-time positions within these zones in the last year, according to the news release.

Founder and CEO Lora Haddock says: <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u201cI am pleased and honored to have a group of investors that prioritize social responsibility along with profit. Our investors represent forward-thinking individuals and organizations that share our vision that the thoughtful application of technology can improve sexual health for millions.  Additionally, our investors support our mission of promoting gender equity and making societal changes that benefit everyone.\u201d
<\/p><\/blockquote>\n\n\n\n

The startup plans to use the investment to help commercialize its first product, the Os\u00e9. The product is the “only product designed for hands-free blended orgasms. Using advanced patent-pending micro-robotics, Os\u00e9 mimics the sensations of a human mouth, tongue, and fingers, for an experience that feels remarkably like a real partner.” <\/p>\n\n\n\n
