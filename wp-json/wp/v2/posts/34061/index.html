Our friends over at BlueBird Strategies have some exciting news to share. The Bend-based demand agency was recently acquired by Square 2 Marketing out of Pennsylvania. The acquisition seems to be a match made in inbound marketing heaven (a special place where all emails are opened, everyone alway clicks on the content and all leads convert).<\/p>\n

Founded by Cari Baldwin, BlueBird Strategies is a Marketo partner and has long served enterprise-level demand generation clients. Meanwhile Square 2 Marketing is HubSpot’s largest partner. Put them together and you have an agency with deep experience and partnerships with two of the most popular marketing automation software platforms, and experience with most of the rest.<\/p>\n

Though the BlueBird team is primarily dispersed, the agency has an office at BendTECH. Half of BlueBird’s 12 employees live and work in Bend, and Baldwin says she’s planning to grow the Oregon team.\u00a0In addition to helming BBS, Baldwin also serves at the chief instructor of BendPoly, which just finished its second year.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe are going to continue \u2018business as usual\u2019 – providing high quality demand generation services to our amazing b2b clients, many of whom we have been working with for years. They will benefit from additional services including website development, and focused inbound marketing including SEO and content creation. The response from clients and partners has been very positive, and the BlueBird team is looking forward to the new opportunities that come with Square 2 Marketing,” Baldwin said via email this morning.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n

She is now the new chief revenue officer at Square 2 Marketing, and her main focus will be retaining and growing her BBS team’s business as well as supporting Square 2 in sales.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n
