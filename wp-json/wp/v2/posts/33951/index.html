Since we last talked to Hueya in March<\/a>, founder Lewis Howell has been hard at work. The former VP of IT at G5 has raised $260,000, hired a small team and is steadily rolling out the first features of the startup’s personal privacy scan product.<\/p>\n

Hueya <\/a>(Who-Ya) aims to help people secure their online privacy through proactive and preventive monitoring. In the current version of the product, you connect your social media accounts, Hueya analyzes the size and contents of your digital footprint and hands you a privacy score.<\/p>\n

The startup has developed a range of 0 to 500 and the higher your score, the less information you have out there floating around, waiting for yahoos to steal it and use it for some nefarious purpose. (My privacy score fell into At Risk this weekend. But I want it to be Awesome).<\/p>\n
