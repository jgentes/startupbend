Why did you leave Sony to start Zero Transform? <\/strong>My two passions are virtual reality and game development. I spent the last 9.5 years making games for Playstation, but I wanted to create a content studio that focuses on making brilliant virtual reality content. This is the evolution of gaming. VR is projected to be a $30 billion industry by 2020 and just under half of that market is VR game content. It\u2019s exciting times for a game developer in VR. And though my experience is primarily in games, VR can help people in so many situations where they want to escape but they can\u2019t. It can give patients in the hospital a chance to be somewhere else or let a person exercising compete with friends or say travel to the top of a volcano<\/p>\n
