The subscription box craze has tapped into a joy universal to all humans: Getting a present in the mail. Is there anything better? Maybe Santa, but only because he brings the presents right into your house. Well, for subscribers of Bend-based StrideBox, Christmas happens once a month.<\/p>\n

The year-and-a-half old company provides a monthly box of treats and new products aimed at runners. We recently caught up with StrideBox founder James Erickson to learn more about his business, why runners make for the best customers and why running — or \u00a0moving around at all — while wearing a giant beaver costume may be the next hot workout.<\/p>\n
