Yesterday we announced that Ignite TAO is presenting 11 tech talks at an event later this month<\/a>. Some of you noticed that of the 11 speakers, only two are women. As one StartupBend reader and growth strategist\/public speaker Kristin Luck<\/a> put it in a tweet, that\u2019s \u201cnot awesome.\u201d<\/p>\n

Nope, it’s not, and the lack of gender diversity in startups and tech remains a significant issue for our community to continue to discuss and address. But before we do, I think it’s important to point out that the Technology Association of Oregon and TAO\u2019s Central Oregon Coordinator Teri Hockett are not only keenly aware of tech\u2019s diversity problem\u2014they\u2019re some of the people working hardest here to tackle it.<\/p>\n

Teri is a driving force behind TAO STEAM<\/a>, a group that launched a few months ago with the specific mission of creating a gender balance in STEAM careers. Their first meeting in June attracted more than a hundred people, and the group\u2019s momentum has been nothing short of inspiring. In fact, the two women speaking at Ignite TAO\u2014Zilaida Salgueiro and Kerri Fry\u2014are founding members of TAO STEAM.<\/p>\n
