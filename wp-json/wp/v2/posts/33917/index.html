Cairn announced today the addition of two local investors: Cascade Angels Fund and Scott Allan, the general manager of Hydro Flask. The new investments bring the startup’s total seed round to $2 million.<\/p>\n

\u201cAs a brand partner, I have experienced Cairn first hand.\u00a0The data they provide to brands greatly informs product\u00a0development, marketing and innovation. At the same time, they are bringing together a unique\u00a0community of consumers, all with a love of the outdoors,\u201d says Allan, who is also joining Cairn’s board of directors.<\/p>\n

Hydro Flask was acquired by Helen of Troy for $210 million in February. Cairn plans to draw on Allan’s experience in creating shareholder value across outdoor products, positioning companies, commercializing technologies, building brands and managing growth.<\/p>\n

Cairn, which provides subscription boxes to outdoor enthusiasts, has grown rapidly since its founding in Bend in 2014, and is a prime example of Central Oregon’s burgeoning outdoor industry. The startup was one of the first Bend Outdoor Worx accelerator graduates and a Bend Venture Conference early stage finalist.<\/p>\n
