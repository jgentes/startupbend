You might have noticed that October has gotten a little busy — and we’re not talking about trying to find just the right pieces for your amazing Harry Potter Halloween costume. In Bend, the best of all fall months is now host to a plethora of independently organized conferences, including Bend Venture, Bend Design, Swivel, the unConference, the Venture Out Festival and BendFilm. Visit Bend has designated the whole to-do as Tenth Month<\/a>, a month-long SXSW-ish celebration of ideas and innovation.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

There’s a lot happening. You can learn about all sorts of things, meet all sorts of interesting people and at the very least, go to some fun parties. It’s like college, but less homework and probably better clothes.<\/p>\n

Here are some of the Tenth Month events to put on your calendar.<\/p>\n

October 6-9 \/\/ BENDFILM FESTIVAL<\/a>
\nThe BendFilm Festival, now in its 13th year, is a celebration of independent\u00a0cinema. The festival is dedicated to supporting brave and talented filmmakers\u00a0while educating communities about the value of independent art. With venues\u00a0sprinkled throughout downtown Bend, you\u2019ll often find the filmmakers hanging\u00a0with the audience.<\/p>\n

October 10-11 \/\/ SWIVEL DIGITAL + CREATIVE MARKETING CONFERENCE<\/a>
\nThe Swivel conference is a two-day boutique marketing education experience\u00a0held at the iconic Tower Theatre, Oxford Hotel, and the DoubleTree by Hilton in\u00a0downtown Bend. Thought leaders from around the globe gather together to\u00a0exchange ideas and how-tos about the latest trends in digital marketing,\u00a0search, social media, and creativity. It\u2019s a must for anyone who conducts\u00a0business via the web and wants to not only keep up with the pace, but stay a
\nstep ahead of the pack.
\nOctober 12 \/\/\u00a0UNCONFERENCE\u00a0<\/a>
\nThis is an informal and fun startup pitchfest, presented by the Tech Alliance of Central Oregon (TACO!) and StartupBend. Startups pitch, the crowd votes and the winner gets to present as the wild card spot on the Bend Venture Conference early stage and a chance to win the $15,000 BendBroadband award.
\nOctober 12 \/\/\u00a0VENTURE OUT FESTIVAL<\/a>
\nThis the first-ever venture event geared solely toward outdoor companies, presented by Bend Outdoor Worx. Early and growth stage startups will pitch and compete for $110,000 in investment and awards. Former professional snowboarder Kevin Pearce is the keynote speaker.<\/p>\n

October 13-14 \/\/ BEND VENTURE CONFERENCE<\/a>
\nThe largest Angel Conference in the Pacific Northwest, Bend Venture\u00a0Conference spotlights entrepreneurship and innovation. Attendees play an\u00a0active role in determining the Early Stage winner, with investments, awards and\u00a0prizes close to $1 million given away at last year\u2019s event.<\/p>\n

October 20-21 \/\/ BEND DESIGN CONFERENCE<\/a>
\nEmbrace creativity in anything and everything. This is the ethos of Bend Design\u00a0Conference. A celebration of design culture in the modern world, the two-day\u00a0event will be filled with conversations, tours, interactive workshops, and hands-on\u00a0exhibits all designed to disrupt the norm.<\/p>\n

Phew. And I’m sure there’s more. You know that saying “You can sleep when you’re dead.” In Bend, you can sleep in November. Because your October is booked.<\/p>\n
