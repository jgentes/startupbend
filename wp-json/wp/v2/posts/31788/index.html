You know that natural gas line that serves your house or small business? What if you could also use it to fuel your car or truck? Onboard Dynamics is aiming to commercialize a technology that provides for natural gas compression on board your vehicle, basically allowing you to refuel anywhere there\u2019s a low-pressure natural gas line.<\/p>\n

So, BBQ some burgers. Then use the same source of gas that fuels your grill to fill up your Ford F-150. BendTECH caught up with Onboard Dynamics CEO Rita Hansen to hear about her whirlwind year of fundraising, OBDI’s recent visit to Detroit and what\u2019s next for this Bend-based startup.<\/p>\n
