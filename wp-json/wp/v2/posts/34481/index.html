As a long-time vegan, Ericka Rodriguez wasn\u2019t pleased with her makeup options, especially when it came to lipstick. \u201cThere was no brand out there that represented what I wanted,\u201d she says. \u201cNothing that was vegan was also hip or luxe and every time I put on a vegan, organic lipstick I didn\u2019t get the result I wanted.\u201d<\/p>\n

Ericka began making her own lipstick in 2012, researching how to make lip balm and then adding color. \u201cIt became an obsession pretty quickly,\u201d she says. \u201cI was working at a juice bar in New York City and making lipstick before work and after work.\u201d<\/p>\n

She landed on a winning formula\u2014and ultimately launched Axiology<\/a>, a luxury vegan, organic lipstick company based here in Bend. The growing brand is now found in 90 boutiques, including Free People shops, around the country.<\/p>\n
