Between equity investments, cash awards and revenue-financing, the region\u2019s largest angel conference doled out $1.65 million in funds after one-and-a-half days of founder pitches.<\/p>\n

The event\u2019s big winner was Bend-based LeadMethod<\/a>, a tech company that makes channel revenue optimization software.
