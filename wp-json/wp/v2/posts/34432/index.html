Cascade Angels is Taking Applications from Startups"},"content":{"rendered":"

If you’re an Oregon-based early-stage startup, then this news is for you. Cascade Angels<\/a>, the Bend-based angel fund, is accepting applications for investment from startups. Entrepreneurs can apply on Gust.com<\/a>\u00a0beginning Monday through March 6.<\/p>\n

The fund expects to invest in four to seven companies in 2017. The typical investments range from $100,000 to $250,000. Now in its fourth year, the\u00a0fund\u2019s mission is to create opportunities for investors and businesses in and with connections to Central Oregon to drive economic growth and fuel prosperity in Oregon, according to a news release. <\/span><\/p>\n

Since 2014, Cascade Angels Fund has committed almost $2 million in 13 companies, which employ over 100 people. In Bend, those companies include Amplion, Droplr, Manzama, Zero Transform, LeadMethod and Odysys.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe continued focus of Cascade Angels is to generate returns for investors, while also generating a return for the community through the impact of job creation and economic diversity,\u201d says Julie Harrelson, Fund Manager for Cascade Angels.<\/p>\n
