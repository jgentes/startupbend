As a former newspaper reporter, I’m a total geek for local government. As it turns out, the actions of your city and county and parks department often have a much larger impact on your daily life than say, what’s happening in Washington, D.C. And next week, you can geek out on local government too, as part of the upcoming TAO Tech Talk featuring Eric King<\/a>, Bend’s city manager.<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Governance<\/li>\n
  • City Services<\/li>\n
  • Council Goals<\/li>\n
  • Finances<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
