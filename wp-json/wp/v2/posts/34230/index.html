If you’ve wanted to work in tech in Bend — or know someone who does — then we have some real opportunities for you. G5 has posted nine open positions in the job board<\/a>, ranging from Chief People Officer and VP of Client Success to a Test Engineer and Creative Accounts Manager.<\/p>\n

The Bend tech company provides digital marketing for real estate companies via its G5 Marketing Cloud platform. The company raised $76 million last year to help accelerate the development and growth of its marketing platform<\/a>\u00a0and buy out some of its early investors.\u00a0Pennsylvania-based Peak Equity Ventures led the round, gaining a majority stake in the company.<\/p>\n

With 225 employees and offices in downtown Bend, the company has been listed on all sorts of good lists<\/a>: Deloitte’s list of fastest growing tech companies; Oregon Business’ Best Places to Work; and Outside Magazine’s Best Places to Work to name a few.<\/p>\n

So if you know someone looking — or perhaps you’re interested in a new job for yourself — take a look. Here’s the listings (you can find them all at startupbend.com\/jobs<\/a>):<\/p>\n

  • Chief People Officer<\/a><\/li>\n
  • VP of Client Success<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Software Engineer–Support<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Test Engineer<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Outbound Marketing Manager<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Creative Account Manager<\/a><\/li>\n
  • Client Engagement Specialist<\/a><\/li>\n
