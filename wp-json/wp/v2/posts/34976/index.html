One of the most fun parts of the Bend Venture Conference is the early stage competition. It features Central Oregon startups who are just getting going, and vying for a $17,500 award to help them launch. Adding to the fun: The audience at this month’s PubTalk, along with an expert panel, gets to weigh in on who moves on to the main conference.<\/p>\n

Today, EDCO announced the semi-finalists, who will pitching to a packed house at\u00a0PubTalk, scheduled for Thursday, Sept. 28th<\/a>.<\/p>\n

In alphabetical order, the\u00a0Ten Early Stage<\/strong>\u00a0semi-finalists are:<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. BladeRunner Energy<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 A micro-hydro technology based on biomimicry that harnesses renewable energy from the natural flow of water.<\/li>\n
  2. kidcareshare<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 A social network for parent communities to instantly schedule and trade carpools, play dates and care.<\/li>\n
  3. Mission Limelight<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 Empowers non-profits and commercial enterprises to maximize their marketing, fundraising and social impact.<\/li>\n
  4. Ockam<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 A blockchain based platform to solve systemic security and interoperability problems in all IOT devices.<\/li>\n
  5. Snappy Sitters<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 Matches reliable and capable sitters with busy families who have extremely high standards.<\/li>\n
  6. Tread & Butter<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 Intentionally-designed cork insoles for sport, work and outdoors.<\/li>\n
  7. Tribe Pilot<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 A social tool to plan outdoor adventure.<\/li>\n
  8. Voil\u00e0<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 Specialty instant coffee from top US roasters, delivered monthly.<\/li>\n
  9. Wildish<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 Makers of outdoorsy gear for outsidey people.<\/li>\n
  10. Wingo<\/a><\/strong>\u00a0\u2013 The first truly ergonomic cases for tablets and smartphones to alleviate pain, prevent injury, and substantially increase comfort.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n
