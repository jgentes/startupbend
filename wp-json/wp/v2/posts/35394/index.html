One of Bend’s own will be part of Project Entrepreneur’s 2018 Class in New York. If you see Jen Ortado, founder of Kidcareshare<\/a>, tell her congrats. She recently discovered she’ll be one of 200 female founders taking part in the April event.<\/p>\n

Project Entrepreneur<\/a> aims to provide\u00a0“women access to the tools, training and networks needed to build scalable, economically impactful companies.” The national initiative was started by the Rent the Runway founders and is supported by UBS. Project Entrepreneur hosts multi-city events, a venture competition, and a 5-week accelerator program.<\/p>\n
