Niki Singlaub travels a lot for work and wanted a water bottle that folded to fit into his pocket while going through airport security. Then he could pop it open and fill it up before boarding. But even after diving deep into the depths of Amazon (there\u2019s things you can\u2019t unsee people) Singlaub couldn\u2019t find what he needed. So being a product designer by trade and an entrepreneur at heart, he made one.<\/p>\n

As it turns out, Singlaub was onto something. His collapsible water bottle not only caught the attention of his friends and family, but he\u2019s raised nearly $100,000 thanks to excited Kickstarter backers this month. And the campaign is still going<\/a>. BendTECH sat down with Singlaub to discuss what goes into designing his collapsible water bottle called the Hydaway, and how he transformed his idea into a successful, nearing six-figure Kickstarter campaign.<\/p>\n
