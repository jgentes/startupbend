Cascade Angels announced today that the fund has invested $100,000 in Bend-based startup LeadMethod (also one of our #50startups<\/a>). LeadMethod\u00a0provides channel sales management software to companies that sell through independent sales reps and distributors.<\/p>\n

From the release:<\/p>\n

The Cascade Angels investment team was impressed with the LeadMethod team and the company\u2019s product, which has already garnered more than a dozen loyal enterprise clients.<\/p>\n

\u201cLeadMethod has targeted an underserved market with a powerful solution that is quickly gaining traction,\u201d says Julie Harrelson, CEO of Harrelson Group, which serves as the Fund Manager for Cascade Angels. \u201cWe\u2019re excited about this recent investment, and it\u2019s great to have local capital available to invest in these opportunities.\u201d<\/p>\n

The Cascade Angels 2015 Fund grew to $725,000 this year, a 58% increase over 2014\u2019s fund. The mission of the Cascade Angels Fund is to create opportunities for investors and businesses in and with connections to Central Oregon to drive economic growth and fuel prosperity across the state.<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

LeadMethod CEO Justin Johnson moved his startup to Bend from the Puget Sound area.<\/p>\n

\u00a0\u201cIt\u2019s great to have the support of local investors as we embark on a period of rapid growth,\u201d Johnson says. \u201cThe Cascade Angels Fund investment certainly helps accelerate our efforts and fuel hires right here in Bend.\u201d<\/p><\/blockquote>\n
