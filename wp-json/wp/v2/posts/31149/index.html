LeadMethod, a Bend-based software company, is looking to build its local development team. The startup specializes in lead management software. LeadMethod<\/a> launched its newest product in January, and has already seen some significant growth.<\/p>\n

“It’s time to take this to the next level, and to do that we need more development resources,” says Justin Johnson*, LeadMethod’s founder and CEO. LeadMethod’s cloud-based software helps clients who use outside sales teams manage and analyze their sales pipeline and performance.<\/p>\n

The company is on the hunt for developers experienced in Java and MongoDB. To spur referrals, the company is offering $1,500 to anyone who recommends a developer candidate that gets hired.<\/p>\n

“The market is really competitive right now and we want to encourage\u00a0people to think of who they may know in their networks,” Johnson says. “We have a strong growth trajectory and this person will be part of a big opportunity.”<\/p>\n
