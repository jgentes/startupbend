By Julie Harrelson, Fund Manager for Cascade Angels<\/p>\n

I was in Kansas City last week representing Oregon at the Kauffman Foundation ESHIP Summit with 430 entrepreneurial community builders including entrepreneurs, investors, educators, government and civic leaders, and economic developers from 48 states and 10 countries. The mission: share best practices to develop and design new knowledge, strategies and tools that will help move entrepreneurial communities\/ecosystems forward. Stoked to be with a diverse group of like-minded ecosystem builders, I wanted to share some of my insights.<\/p>\n
