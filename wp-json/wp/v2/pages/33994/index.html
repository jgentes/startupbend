The StartupBend audience consists of about 2,000 people throughout the Northwest, growing about 10% each month, with most of our traffic coming from Oregon and California.<\/p>\n

The demographic is generally higher income, 30+ professionals, with a mix of entrepreneurs, investors, mentors, and telecommuters. <\/p>\n

Many readers are likely candidates to move to Bend. We generally see a few new people in the coworking space each month that have moved from California or elsewhere, and they often cite the blog as a resource they used to keep tabs on the startup community and search for jobs.<\/p>\n

Our newsletter goes out with every new post (usually a few times each week) and it drives the readers back to the website, where they will see your ad next to the article.<\/p>\n

The newsletter ads are 200w x 200h and the website ads are 300w x 200h.<\/p>\n
